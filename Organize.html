Senat Mahasiswa Fakultas

The Faculty Student Senate is an organization that operates within the university, led by the University Student Senate. The purpose of the Faculty Student Senate (SMF) is to organize various activities that support the development of all students in the faculty. I joined the SMF as the General Secretary, responsible for supervising, evaluating, creating, and removing proposals or reports for all activities conducted during the 2022 period.

XT Radio

XT Radio is a local radio station in Salatiga, established by students from the Faculty of Electronics and Computer Engineering as a platform for student expression. I am a member of the XT Radio community, where my role involves maintaining the electronic systems used for broadcasting. In addition to ensuring the equipment runs smoothly, I also collaborate with fellow members to develop engaging content and enhance the overall quality of our broadcasts. This experience has not only allowed me to apply my technical skills but also to contribute to the vibrant student culture in our community.

PETROS

Petros (Prayer Fellowship of Electronics and Computer Engineering) is a form of student expression reflecting our longing to serve and engage in community fellowship. I am a member responsible for coordinating speakers and preachers to participate in our Petros activities. In this role, I not only arrange schedules and topics but also work to create a welcoming environment for both the speakers and attendees. This experience has deepened my commitment to service and strengthened my connections within our community, allowing us to grow together in faith and understanding.
